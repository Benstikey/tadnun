## The Hidden Cost of Missed Calls

It's 10 PM on a Tuesday. A woman in Casablanca decides she wants a hammam session and massage for Saturday morning. She searches Google, finds your spa, and calls. No answer -- you closed at 8 PM. She tries again the next day during her lunch break. The line is busy because your receptionist is already on another call. By the time you call back, she's booked with your competitor down the street.

This scenario plays out dozens of times every week in Moroccan hammams and spas. Most wellness businesses rely entirely on phone calls and walk-ins for bookings. The result: lost revenue during evenings, weekends, and holidays -- exactly when potential clients are most likely to search and book.

A simple online booking system changes everything. Your clients book when it suits them, you wake up to a full schedule, and you never hire a full-time receptionist just to answer the phone.

## What Online Booking Actually Looks Like for a Hammam

Forget complicated websites or expensive software. For a Moroccan hammam or spa, an effective booking system needs just four things:

### 1. A Service Menu with Prices

List every service you offer with clear pricing in MAD. Be specific:

- Hammam traditionnel -- 150 MAD (45 min)
- Hammam + Gommage -- 250 MAD (60 min)
- Massage relaxant -- 300 MAD (60 min)
- Forfait complet (Hammam + Gommage + Massage) -- 500 MAD (2h)

When clients see prices upfront, they book faster. No back-and-forth messages asking "Combien le hammam?"

### 2. Real-Time Availability

The system shows which time slots are open and which are taken. No double-bookings, no confusion. If you have 3 hammam cabins, the system knows that and only accepts 3 simultaneous bookings per slot.

### 3. Instant Confirmation via WhatsApp

After booking, the client receives a WhatsApp confirmation with the date, time, service, and total price. No phone call needed. No "let me check and get back to you."

### 4. Automated Reminders

24 hours before the appointment, the client gets a WhatsApp reminder: "Reminder: your hammam + massage is tomorrow at 10:00. Reply YES to confirm or call us to reschedule." This single message can cut your no-show rate in half.

## Setting Up WhatsApp Business as Your Booking Channel

You don't need a custom app or an expensive platform to start. WhatsApp Business -- which is free -- can handle a significant portion of your booking workflow.

### Step 1: Create Your Business Profile

Download WhatsApp Business (separate from regular WhatsApp). Fill in your spa's name, address, opening hours, and a description of your services. Add professional photos of your hammam, treatment rooms, and products.

### Step 2: Build Your Catalog

WhatsApp Business lets you create a product catalog. Add each service as a "product" with a photo, description, duration, and price. Clients can browse your offerings directly in the chat -- like a digital menu.

### Step 3: Set Up Automatic Replies

Configure these three messages:

- **Welcome message**: "Welcome to [Spa Name]! Browse our services in the catalog below, or tell us what you'd like to book. We respond within 15 minutes during business hours."
- **Away message**: "Thank you for your message! We're currently closed but will respond first thing in the morning. To book online anytime, visit [booking link]."
- **Quick replies**: Pre-save answers to your most common questions -- pricing, hours, location, parking information.

### Step 4: Use Labels to Track Bookings

Tag every conversation: "New inquiry," "Booking confirmed," "Deposit paid," "Completed." This turns WhatsApp into a basic CRM for your spa.

## Accepting Deposits to Reduce No-Shows

No-shows are the biggest profit killer in the wellness industry. A client who doesn't show up for a 2-hour hammam package at 500 MAD doesn't just cost you 500 MAD -- it costs you the client you could have booked in that slot.

The solution: require a deposit at the time of booking.

### How to Collect Deposits in Morocco

**CMI Payment Links**: If you have a CMI merchant account, you can generate a payment link and send it directly in the WhatsApp conversation. The client pays 100-200 MAD as a deposit, and you send a confirmation. Setting up a CMI merchant account costs approximately 1,500-3,000 MAD in initial fees, with transaction commissions around 1.5-2.5%.

**CashPlus or Wafacash**: For clients who prefer cash, they can make a deposit at any CashPlus or Wafacash point using a reference number you provide. This is particularly useful for clients outside major cities.

**Bank Transfer**: A simple virement to your business account works for larger packages (bridal hammam, group bookings). Share your RIB via WhatsApp.

## The Seasonal Opportunity You're Missing

Moroccan hammams and spas see massive demand spikes around weddings (June-September), Eid, and Ramadan. During these periods, a spa without online booking is leaving thousands of dirhams on the table.

### Wedding Season Packages

Create bookable packages specifically for brides and bridal parties:

- Bridal hammam package (bride + 4 guests) -- 3,000 MAD
- Pre-wedding glow package (3 sessions over 2 weeks) -- 1,800 MAD
- Bridal party day (hammam + massage + tea service) -- 5,000 MAD

Make these bookable online with a 50% deposit. Brides plan weeks in advance -- they want to lock in their date and stop worrying about it.

### Ramadan Evening Slots

During Ramadan, demand shifts to evening hours. Update your online availability to reflect extended evening hours and ftour-compatible scheduling. Clients searching "hammam Casablanca soir Ramadan" should find you with available evening slots ready to book.
