In Morocco, WhatsApp is everywhere. Your customers use it to ask questions, request a price, book an appointment. But if you're managing everything from your personal number -- between family messages, group chats, and vacation photos -- you're losing messages. And every lost message is a lost customer.

## What WhatsApp Business Changes for Your Company

### A Professional Profile That Builds Trust

With WhatsApp Business, you display a real company profile: name, address, hours, website, description of your services. Your customers immediately see they're dealing with a professional, not an unknown number.

### A Product and Service Catalog

No more sending photos one by one. The WhatsApp Business catalog lets you showcase your products with photos, descriptions, and prices. Your customer browses your offerings directly in the conversation, like a pocket storefront. For a retail business, it's a mini sales site accessible without any additional app.

### Automatic Replies That Work for You

How many times have you answered "Yes, we're open" or "Here are our rates"? WhatsApp Business automatic replies save time:

- **Welcome message**: greet every new contact with a professional message
- **Away message**: let your customers know when you're not available
- **Quick replies**: save your most frequent responses and send them in one tap

For an SME receiving 30 to 50 messages a day, that's easily an hour saved every evening.

### WhatsApp Business API: Scaling Up

For companies that want to go further, the WhatsApp Business API lets you integrate WhatsApp directly into your business tools. Automatic confirmations, delivery notifications, appointment reminders, sales follow-ups -- everything is automated and personalized. Tadnun integrates the WhatsApp Business API into its solutions to connect messaging to your CRM, booking system, or inventory management.

## Real-World Use Cases Across Every Sector

### Restaurants: Frictionless Reservations and Orders

A customer sends "Table for 4 tonight?" and receives an automatic confirmation with the time and address. The catalog displays the daily menu. Reminders reduce no-shows. It's simple, fast, and speaks to everyone.

### Retail: Catalog and Automatic Replies

A clothing store in Casablanca shares its WhatsApp catalog with the week's new arrivals. Customers browse, ask questions, and order -- all in a single conversation. Automatic replies handle questions about sizes and opening hours.

### Healthcare: Appointment Reminders

A clinic loses an average of 2,000 MAD per day due to patients who don't show up. With the WhatsApp Business API, every patient receives a reminder 24 hours before their appointment, with a button to confirm or reschedule. No-shows drop, the schedule fills up.

A real estate agent receives inquiries from Avito, Mubawab, and Facebook. Without a system, half the leads are lost. The sales agent picks up the conversation at the right time, with full context.

By integrating CMI (Centre Monetique Interbancaire) with WhatsApp Business via the API, your customers can receive a payment link directly in the conversation. No need for a full e-commerce site. Your customer sees the product in the catalog, asks their questions, and pays -- without leaving WhatsApp. Tadnun configures this CMI integration for its clients to simplify the purchasing journey.

## How to Get Started

1. **Download WhatsApp Business** from the App Store or Google Play -- it's free
2. **Complete your profile**: company name, address, hours, description, website
3. **Create your catalog**: add your 10 most requested products or services with photos and prices
4. **Set up automatic replies**: welcome message, away message, 5 quick replies for frequently asked questions
5. **Organize your contacts**: use labels (new customer, order in progress, VIP) so you never lose track
6. **Move to the API** when you're ready: to automate at scale, integrate your CRM, and activate CMI payments
