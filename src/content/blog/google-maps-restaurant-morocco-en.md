## Step 1: Create or Claim Your Google Business Listing

1. Go to [Google Business Profile](https://business.google.com)
2. Search for your restaurant's name
3. If a listing already exists, claim it. Otherwise, create one.
4. Fill in all the information: exact address, phone number, opening hours, website

**Tadnun Tip**: Use your WhatsApp Business number as your primary number -- it's the channel your customers prefer in Morocco.

## Step 2: Professional Photos

Google favors listings with recent, high-quality photos. Upload at minimum:

- 5 photos of your signature dishes
- 3 photos of the interior and terrace
- 1 photo of the storefront (helps customers find you)
- 1 photo of the team (builds trust)

**Tip**: Tadnun includes a professional photography service as part of its restaurant packages.

Reviews are the #1 ranking factor on Google Maps. Tadnun sets up a simple system:

3. You receive a WhatsApp alert for every new review

## Step 4: Post Regularly

Google favors active listings. Post once a week:

- The daily special (with a photo)
- Special offers
- Events (themed evenings, Ramadan ftour...)

- **Positive reviews**: Thank the customer by name and mention the dish they highlighted
