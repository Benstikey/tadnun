# Social Content Plan -- Tadnun

*Last updated: 2026-03-27*

---

## 1. Platform Strategy

### LinkedIn -- B2B Decision-Makers
- **Frequency:** 3x/week (Mardi, Jeudi, Samedi)
- **Tone:** Professional, data-driven, thought-leadership
- **Content types:** Pain point posts with MAD figures, ROI stats, before/after workflows, sector case studies, founder insights
- **Format:** Text posts (1200-1500 chars), carousels (5-8 slides), document posts (PDF one-pagers)
- **Best posting times:** 8h00-9h00 and 12h00-13h00 (Casablanca time)
- **Goal:** Position Tadnun as the authority on Moroccan SME digitalization; generate qualified leads from decision-makers

### Facebook -- Broad Moroccan Reach
- **Frequency:** 4x/week (Lundi, Mercredi, Vendredi, Dimanche)
- **Tone:** Conversational, relatable, warm -- speak like a trusted neighbor
- **Content types:** Pain point stories, testimonial cards, behind-the-scenes, educational tips, urgency posts (2030 World Cup)
- **Format:** Image posts, short videos (<60s), carousel albums, polls/questions
- **Best posting times:** 12h00-14h00 and 19h00-21h00
- **Goal:** Build awareness and trust; drive WhatsApp conversations; reach SME owners who browse casually

### Instagram -- Visual Sectors
- **Frequency:** 3x/week (Mardi, Jeudi, Samedi) + Stories daily
- **Tone:** Visual-first, aspirational before/after, clean design
- **Content types:** Before/after visuals, quote cards, ROI stat graphics, Reels (30-60s sector walkthroughs), carousel tips
- **Format:** Square (1080x1080) for feed, 9:16 for Reels/Stories
- **Best posting times:** 12h00-13h00 and 18h00-20h00
- **Goal:** Showcase visual transformation for restaurants, tourism, retail; build brand aesthetic; drive link-in-bio traffic

## 2. Content Categories & Ratios

| Category | Ratio | Description | Platforms |
|----------|-------|-------------|-----------|
| Pain Point Posts | 30% | Sector-specific problems with real MAD figures and emotional hooks | LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram |
| Before/After | 20% | Workflow transformations showing old vs. new process | LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook |
| Testimonial Cards | 15% | Named quotes with role and city -- social proof | Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn |
| ROI Stats | 15% | Visual stat graphics with bold numbers | LinkedIn, Instagram |
| Behind-the-Scenes | 10% | Team culture, on-site visits, Moroccan values, process photos | Facebook, Instagram |
| Educational Tips | 10% | Actionable guides, how-to content, sector tips | LinkedIn, Facebook |

### Weekly Distribution (40 posts/month total)

- LinkedIn (12 posts): 4 Pain Point, 2 Before/After, 2 ROI Stats, 2 Educational, 1 Testimonial, 1 Behind-the-Scenes
- Facebook (16 posts): 5 Pain Point, 3 Testimonial, 3 Before/After, 2 Educational, 2 Behind-the-Scenes, 1 ROI Stats
- Instagram (12 posts): 3 Before/After, 3 ROI Stats, 2 Pain Point, 2 Testimonial, 1 Behind-the-Scenes, 1 Educational

## 4. Repurposing Flows

| Source Element | Post Type | Platform | How to Extract |
|----------------|-----------|----------|----------------|
| Main thesis/hook | Pain Point post | LinkedIn | Take the opening problem statement, add real MAD figure, end with question |
| Key statistic | ROI stat graphic | Instagram | Isolate the single most compelling number, design visual card |
| Customer quote | Testimonial card | Facebook | Pull quote with name/role/city, add 1-2 supporting stats below |

## 6. Content Production Notes

### Visual Design Guidelines
- **Colors:** Warm neutrals (cream, beige), accents in teal/green (success), red (pain/before), brand colors
- **Typography:** Fraunces for headlines on graphics, Plus Jakarta Sans for body text
- **Photo style:** Authentic Moroccan business settings, warm natural light, no stock photos
- **Data visuals:** Bold numbers, minimal chart decoration, always in DH (not MAD on French posts)
- **Format consistency:** All quote cards same template, all ROI cards same template, all before/after same split layout

### Content Calendar Management
- **Batch creation:** Produce all 4 weeks of content in 2 production sessions
- **Approval workflow:** Draft -> Review for accuracy (MAD figures, testimonial names) -> Visual design -> Schedule
- **Scheduling tool:** Buffer or Hootsuite for cross-platform scheduling
- **Engagement window:** 30 minutes after each post to respond to comments
- **Performance review:** Weekly -- track engagement rate, link clicks, WhatsApp conversations initiated
