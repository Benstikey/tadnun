# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-03-27*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Tadnun builds custom digital solutions for Moroccan businesses — tailored to local sectors, integrations, and language.
**What it does:** Helps Moroccan SMEs digitalize their operations with sector-specific tools (CRM, POS, booking systems, traceability platforms, etc.) that integrate with local services (CMI, ONSSA, CNSS, Glovo, WhatsApp Business) and work in French, Darija, and Arabic.
**Product category:** Digital transformation services / custom business software
**Product type:** Service (consultancy + custom development + ongoing support)
**Business model:** Custom pricing per project, tailored to each client's size, sector, and needs. Includes setup, training, and ongoing support. No hidden fees.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Moroccan SMEs with <20 employees (80% of client base)
**Decision-makers:** Business owners, cooperative presidents, clinic directors, hotel/riad owners, restaurant owners
**Primary use case:** Replace manual/paper processes with digital tools that integrate with Moroccan platforms
**Jobs to be done:**
- Stop losing money to inefficiency (missed contracts, manual errors, invisible waste)
- Become visible online (Google Maps, reviews, delivery platforms)
- Automate daily operations (bookings, inventory, billing, follow-ups)
**Use cases:**
- Agriculture cooperatives needing export traceability (ONSSA compliance)
- Restaurants needing Google presence + delivery order management
- Hotels/riads reducing Booking.com commissions via direct booking sites
- Healthcare clinics automating appointment reminders + AMO/CNSS billing
- Retailers connecting POS, stock tracking, and WhatsApp catalog
- Schools digitizing enrollment, grades, and parent communication
- Real estate agencies managing leads across Avito, Mubawab, Facebook
- Logistics companies replacing paper delivery slips with digital tracking

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** 93% of Moroccan SMEs have no digital presence. They run on paper, WhatsApp groups, and gut feelings — losing money, customers, and contracts they don't even know about.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Foreign SaaS (HubSpot, Zoho): expensive, not localized — no Darija, no CMI, no ONSSA integration
- Local web agencies / freelancers: build websites but don't solve operational problems
- Odoo integrators: too complex, enterprise-priced, overkill for SMEs
**What it costs them:** 400K MAD lost export contracts, 300K MAD/year in Booking.com commissions, 2,000 MAD/day in no-show patients, 15-20% weekly food waste
**Emotional tension:** Fear of being left behind as competitors digitalize, especially with the 2030 World Cup approaching. Frustration with processes that waste hours every day. Doubt about whether technology is "for them."

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Odoo integrators in Morocco — falls short because too complex, too expensive, enterprise-focused, not sector-specific
**Secondary:** Local web agencies / freelancers — falls short because they build websites but don't solve operational pain points (no CRM, no POS, no integrations)
**Indirect:** Foreign SaaS (HubSpot, Zoho, generic tools) — falls short because no MAD pricing, no Darija interface, no local integrations (CMI, ONSSA, CNSS), no on-site support

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Built for Morocco: Darija/French interfaces, CMI payment, WhatsApp Business, ONSSA/CNSS/CNDP compliance
- Simple to use: "If you can use WhatsApp, you can use Tadnun" — icons, voice input, no tech expertise needed
- Human support: On-site training in Darija/French, dedicated contact, WhatsApp support
- Sector expertise: Deep knowledge of 8 sectors' workflows, pain points, and local integrations
**How we do it differently:** We visit the business first (eat at the restaurant, walk the fields, sit in the clinic). We understand the workflow before writing code. We build iteratively with feedback at every step.
**Why that's better:** Solutions fit the actual business, not a generic template. Staff adopt tools because they were designed around their real workflow.
**Why customers choose us:** Locality (we speak Darija, we know CMI, we come on-site), simplicity (no IT expertise needed), and the fact that we solve operations — not just build websites.

**Anti-persona:** Large enterprises with in-house IT teams. Businesses outside Morocco. Companies looking for off-the-shelf SaaS without customization. Businesses not ready to change their processes at all.

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Paper processes losing real money (400K MAD contracts, 300K MAD commissions). Competitors going digital. 2030 World Cup approaching — businesses without digital presence "simply won't exist."
**Pull:** Local integrations no one else offers (ONSSA, CMI, CNSS). Darija support. Affordable custom pricing. Human on-site support. Sector-specific solutions that feel built for them.
**Habit:** "We've always done it this way." Comfort with paper notebooks and WhatsApp groups. Fear that change will disrupt daily operations.
**Anxiety:** "My staff can't handle technology." Cost concerns. Fear of being locked into a contract. Uncertainty about whether digitalization is "worth it" for a small business.
